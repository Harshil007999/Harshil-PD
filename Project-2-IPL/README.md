## Project 2 — IPL Match Analysis

### About
Analysis of IPL cricket matches from 2008-2020 using Python and Pandas.

### Questions Answered
1. Total matches played
2. Team with most wins
3. Matches per season
4. City that hosted most matches
5. Does toss winner also win the match?
6. Most popular toss decision
7. Most player of the match awards
8. Most popular venue
9. Biggest win by runs
10. Team that won most tosses

### Tools Used
- Python, Pandas
